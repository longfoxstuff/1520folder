WE'RE ALL EARS

We're here for you and we're all ears. it's your job to keep us on our toes. If you have a question, suggestion or bone to pick please send an email message, call us on the phone or put pen to paper and let us know what's on your mind. Our goal at SNC is to provide the community with an abundant supply of things to see and do all year long - performances, art exhibits, instructional classes and historical displays that expand your personal horizons and make life richer in so many ways. We also recognize our larger responsibility in reaching out to our neighboring Northeast Georgia communities to share the best we have to offer and be just that, good neighbors. So take advantage of what we have to offer, support us in whatever ways are appropriate for you and become part of the larger Sautee Nacoochee / Northest Georgia Community. But most of all stay in touch!
